01
Two roles to fill

One brand, two jobs.

Job 1 · Paid & controlled

Brand-awareness content

  • We pay creators → we own the brief, messaging & framing.
  • Content stays on Halio's campaign message.
  • Home: Shopify Collabs.
"Control the story."
Job 2 · Organic affiliate

Low-cost incremental sales

  • No flat fee — creators post on their own for commission.
  • Adds sales without steering the brand story.
  • Home: LTK (or ShopMy).
"Cheap incremental reach."

Conclusion · final call

Run both.

Primary · 75% · Paid

Shopify Collabs

  • Paid, controlled — CPM + Round-up + Paid Collab.
  • Keeps content on Halio's awareness message.
"10% · all products + 10% off selected."
Secondary · 25% · Organic

LTK

  • Free, video-first, 44M high-intent shoppers.
  • Pure incremental sales.
"15% · full-price products only."

Reserve → keep ShopMy for premium / derma plays on high-AOV (~$1,000) products later — not now.
