How We Measure · North Star & Method

No invented numbers — one metric, tracked first-party.

North Star
First-party-attributed NMV per asset — the net revenue each post drives.

Views and saves matter only because they move this. We don't celebrate reach that doesn't reach the cart.

Tracked, not estimated

Attribution

LTK first-party clicks→orders + a unique code per creator + UTMs.

Formula

Blended ROAS

attributed NMV ÷ total creator + media cost

Honesty

What's estimated

View-to-sale halo is marked ≈ estimate, never blended into hard ROAS.
