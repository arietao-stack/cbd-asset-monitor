⚠️ Reality check on the organic layer: creators do not post a dedicated video for every product. On commission-only they most often just add your product to their shop / a collection — a real video comes only when they genuinely like it and the commission is worth their time (Halio's high AOV helps; CoBa's $100 AOV is a hard sell). Even LTK's paid Quick Collabs gives creators full creative control — no brief, no approval; true briefing only exists in LTK's $50K managed campaigns. So controlled, on-brief content lives in the paid Shopify Collabs track — that's exactly why it's primary.
